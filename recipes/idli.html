Idli

Ingredients

  • Idli Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Curry Leaves
  • Ghee
  1. Mix idli rice, water, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Let the batter ferment for some time.
  3. Add some curry leaves and pour out the batter into idli moulds.
  4. Steam the moulds and you have idlis that are ready to serve!
