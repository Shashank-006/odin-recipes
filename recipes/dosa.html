Dosa

Ingredients for Batter:

  • Urad dahl
  • Chana Dahl
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  1. Grind all of the ingredients together to make the batter.
  2. Let the batter ferment overnight.
  3. Put the stove on medium and spread the batter in a circular motion
  4. When crispy, take off the stove and it's ready to serve!
